My third single, Gone For Long, describes the passing of my sweet late grandmother. I was in London at the time preparing to record at Abbey Road Studios with the Angel City Chorale. While eating lunch with my friend Karol, I recieved a phone call from the U.S. with the horrible news. Thankfully my mom was with my grandmother at the time, and she died in my mom's arms.

This song is in turn dedicated to her.

The music video accompanying this track was inspired (and entirely constrained) by the shelter-in-place restrictions due to the 2020 novel Coronavirus. We were hoping to express loneliness and isolation through this piece, while also depicting an attempt to keep one's internal fire alive despite the circumstances

This music video is my first (!) of hopefully many more to come.

I hope you enjoy it!

-L
