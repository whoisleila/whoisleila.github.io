Bio

Hailing from Neverheardofit Pennsylvania, Leila’s indie sound and lyrics carry their humble roots. A little jazz, a little soul, a hint of strange, and three shots of romance will keep this spellbinding cocktail of uncanny ballads in your head forever.

Leila describes her music as Subtle Freak Hawaiian Prom, heavily influenced by the romance and quirks of vintage Italian film scores. As you step into LeilaLand, a syrupy vocal and hypnotic wave of pulsating guitars and synths wash you away from wherever you’re sitting and into a daydream.

Her debut album Glass Highways is scheduled for release Fall 2020 on all digital platforms and vinyl. This project is entirely self funded by Leila through her full-time software engineering job.

She’d love to hear from you. Please reach out to contact@leilaland.com
